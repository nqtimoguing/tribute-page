Blessed Carlo Acutis

A millenial saint.

Carlo Acutis was an English-born Catholic youth, a computer programmer and web developer/designer who created his own website where he cataloged all the Eucharistic miracles that takes place from all over the world.

The Timeline of Carlo's Life

  • May 3, 1991- Carlo was born in London United Kingdom, where his parents were employed.
  • May 18, 1991- He was baptised in the church of Our Lady of Dolours in Chelsea central London.
  • September 8, 1991 - His parents Andrea Acutis and Antonia Salzano settled in Milan, Italy where Carlo was raised.
  • May 3, 1995 - He began kindergarten at the municipal school of Parco Pagano in Milan.
  • 1997 - He started primary school at the intitusi Tommaseo, of the Marcellinas nuns.
  • June 16, 1998- He receive his first communion in the convent of the Hermit Nuns of San Abrosio, in Perego.
  • May 24, 2003- He receives the Sacrament of Confirmation in his parish, Santa Maria Segreta
  • Sept 2005- He began his higher education at the Leon XIII Institute of the Jesuits Fathers.
  • October 6, 2005- He was diagnosed with a fulmenant leukemia disease with type M3.
  • October 10, 2006Asked to receive the sacrament of Annointing of the Sick.
  • October 12, 2006- At 6:45 a.m. his heart stopped beating and was pronounced dead.
  • January 2007- His body was transferred from the Ternengo cemetery to Assisi cemetery.
